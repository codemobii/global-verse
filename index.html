The Mansa's

The Mansa’s are a unique & generative collectable art of 10,000 NFT’s. The premise the Mansas dates to the 1300’s. Mansa Musa is widely believed to be the wealthiest African that ever lived. It is said that as he travelled from Africa to the middle east countries felt his presence with the amount of Gold he travelled with.

The Mansa’s are designed to have native African styles, pop art styles and other amazing generative art concepts designed by our amazing artist under the Global Verse.

The Jub's

The backbone that no one spoke about behind the Mansa’s were the Jubi’s. The Jubi’s were the foundation. They use their mystical powers to create vast wealth and protection for the Mansa’s.

The Jubi’s used the Gold dust for Magic and Alchemy. The Spice was used for healing and protecting the Mansa’s tribe. It was also used for predicting the future as the Mansa’s travelled across Africa to other lands around the world.
